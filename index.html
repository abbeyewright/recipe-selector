French Toast Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 6-8 slices Bread
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 2-3 Tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla flavoring
  • Syrup
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

Whisk eggs, vanilla milk, cinnamon, and salt together until smooth. Place a pan over medium heat and add about a Tbsp of butter to melt. Take a slice of bread and dip it into the egg mixture. Let is soak for about 2-3 seconds per side. Place the soaked bread into the hot pan. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side. Serve with syrup. Enjoy!

Beef Enchiladas

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 8 Tortillas
  • 1 can Tomato Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese, shredded
  • 1 can Olives
  • 1 cup Salsa

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F and prepare a 9x13 baking dish with nonstick cooking spray or butter. Brown the ground beef in a large pan over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat and return meat to pan. Stir in the salsa and cook until heated through. Remove from heat. Pour half tomato sauce in the bottom of prepared pan. Warm tortillas in pan. Add about 1/4 cup of meat mixture down the center of each tortilla and top with cheese and olives. Tightly roll each tortilla up and place them in the dish. Pour remaining tomato sauce over them and sprinkle cheese over them. Tightly wrap the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 30 to 35 mins.

Roast Beef with Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 Beef Roast, thawed
  • 1.5 lbs Potatoes, cut in half
  • Onion, chopped
  • 2 cups water or broth

Instructions

Preheat oven to 300°F. Pat beef roast dry with paper towels and season generously on all sides with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven or similar pan/pot over medium-high heat. Add the roast and sear it to 4-5 mins per side until a rich, brown crust forms. Remove the beef to a plate. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and heat for 3 mins. Pour in water or broth. Turn off the heat, return the seared beef to the pot and arrange the potatoes around and on top of the meat. Cover the pot with a lid and place it in the oven. Let is slow-roast for 3-4 hours, or until the beef is fork-tender and pulls apart easily. Transfer the beef and potatoes to a platter to rest for 10-15 mins before serving.

  • 3 Tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 375°F. Rub your chicken pieces generously with seasonings of your choice. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, stir together the dry rice, broth, melted butter, and diced onion. Hamburger Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1-2 can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup ribbon noodles
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/4 cup milk

Instructions

Cook ground beef in a pan until browned. 5-7 mins. Drain excess grease. Mix tomato sauce and seasonings of choice and simmer for about 20 mins. Remove from heat and cover the pan. While the sauce is cooking, bring pot of water to boil and cook noodles. While noodles are cooking, preheat oven to 350 degrees and grease a 9x13 inch casserole dish. Mix sour cream and onion together in a bowl. Layer 1/2 of the noodles into the dish. Top with 1/2 of the sour cream mixture, then 1/2 of the ground beef mixture. Repeat again and sprinkle cheddar cheese over top. Bake in the oven until cheese is melted and golden brown, 25-30 mins. Enjoy!

Rice & Beans Meal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cups water or broth

Instructions

Heat some oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 mins. Add seasonings of choice to the pot. Stir to toast the spices. Pour rice into the pot. Stir for about 2 minutes until the edges of the rice become translucent. Stir in the rinsed beans, broth/water, and salt. Bring the mixture to a boil. Immediately cover the pot with a lid and reduce the heat to low simmer. Let is cook undisturbed for 15-18 mins. Remove the pot from the heat and let it sit covered for 5 mins. Enjoy!

Ground Beef & Potato Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb potatoes, diced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded

Instructions

Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the potatoes in a single layer and cook undisturbed until lightly browned underneath, about 2 minutes. Stir and then cook undisturbed to brown them some more for another 2 minutes. Move potatoes to one side of the pan and add the ground beef to other side of the pan. Sprinkle the potatoes and the beef with salt and pepper. Cook the beef until done. On top of the potatoes and beef, add the onion, bell pepper, and seasonings of choice. Flip and stir until everything is evenly mixed together. Cook until vegetables are softened, beef is no longer pink anywhere, and potatoes are fork-tender. About 3-4 minutes. Turn off heat. Sprinkle cheese and allow steam to melt it. Enjoy!

Instructions

Remove the potatoes from the pan and set aside. Add the sausage, onion and bell pepper into the pan and cook until the vegetables are tender, about 5 mins. Add the sausage back in and cook for a couple of minutes. Enjoy!

Chicken & Rice Skillet

Ingredients

  • 1 can Chicken Breast Pieces
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp butter
  • 1/2 lb carrots, chopped
  • 4 oz. cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 3 cups water/broth

Instructions

Dice the onion and add it to an oven-safe pan along with the butter. Cook over medium heat until the onions are soft. Add chicken to the pan and season to your liking. Add water/broth to pan, add the rice, and stir. Plat a lid on the pan, turn heat up to medium-high, and allow the broth to come to a boil. Once it boils, turn the heat down to low and let the pan simmer for 15 mins. Stir in the carrots, and let it sit over medium-low heat for another 5 mins. Turn on the oven's broiler, top the pan with shredded cheese, and place the pan in the oven under the broiler for a few minutes to melt the cheese. Enjoy!
